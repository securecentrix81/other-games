Game Overview
This is a survival RPG where you play as Eric, a student trapped in a nightmare version of his school. Your goal is to survive, pass your classes by leveling up your "Grade", and avoid the relentless Teacher who chases you.
Features
  • Dungeon-Crawler Gameplay: Explore 5 unique zones (Hallway, Classroom, Library, Cafeteria, Gym).
  • Turn-Based Combat: Fight "Stress Monsters", "Homework", and Rivals using a menu-based battle system.
  • RPG Mechanics:
    • Level Up: Gaining XP (Grades) increases your stats and max HP/MP.
    • Inventory: Pick up Energy Drinks (Heal), Pencils (Weapon), and other items.
    • Skills: Use MP to cast "Homework Strike", "Study Shield", and more.
  • Dynamic Threats:
    • The Teacher: A persistent boss entity that patrols the Hallway. If he catches you in the overworld, you get Detention (Game Over). He gets faster every time you level up.
    • Rivals: Anbu, Saharsh, and Calvin act as mini-bosses in specific rooms.
  • Visual Polish: Screen shake, damage numbers, flash effects, and CRT scanlines for a retro horror vibe.
How to Play
  1. Movement: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move Eric.
  2. Interact: Press SPACE to talk to NPCs, open doors, or pick up items.
  3. Combat:
    • Fight: Standard attack.
    • Skill: Uses MP for special effects (Heavy damage, Heal, etc.).
    • Item: Uses an item from your inventory (e.g., Energy Drink).
    • Run: Attempt to flee (fails against Bosses).
  4. Goal: Survive, defeat the Teacher in the Gym (Boss Fight), or reach Level 5 (Grade A) to pass.
Technical Details
  • Built with HTML5 Canvas for rendering.
  • Tailwind CSS for UI overlays.
  • Procedural-style Map Generation for item placement.
  • Saharsh Integration: Includes the specific requested image for the rival character Saharsh in the Library.
