Game overview
  • Core: A single-page, feature-complete romance + strategy dating sim where you balance actions across Morning/Noon/Evening phases to deepen bonds with Saharsh and Anbu.
  • Loop: Each phase allows one major action (Talk, Gift, Study, Puzzle). Your choices raise Affection, Trust, Charm, and Intellect. Reach Affection and Trust ≥ 80 by Day 20 to unlock the mutual confession ending.
  • Systems: Inventory and gift shop, campus map location effects, journal logging, milestones, multiple endings, save/load, difficulty modes, and a puzzle mini-game.
Controls
  • Space: Advance
  • Enter: Select first visible choice
  • Esc: Pause
  • Arrow keys: Navigate choices
  • Buttons: Actions, shop, map, save/load
How to play
  • Phases: Each day has Morning, Noon, and Evening. Use one major action per phase to shape your relationship stats.
  • Talk: Choose your tone. Honest dialogue raises Trust; playful charm boosts Affection. The focused character reacts differently.
  • Gift: Buy items in the shop, then offer them. Each gift adds unique stat bonuses. Manage coins—study and puzzles help earn/offset costs.
  • Study: Increases Intellect and a bit of Trust. Also grants a few coins.
  • Puzzle: Solve the word tile mini-game (“KISSING”) for a burst of Intellect, Charm, and Affection. Shuffle tiles or give up if stuck.
  • Map: Visit locations (Old Tree, Library, Courtyard, Café) for small, themed stat boosts and flavor journal entries.
Progress and endings
  • Milestones: Trigger at key stat thresholds (First Flutter, Steady Steps, Spark of Insight, Hearts Aligned).
  • Endings:
    • True End: Affection & Trust ≥ 80 by Day 20.
    • Bittersweet End: One major stat high without full alignment.
    • Quiet End: Low stats at the deadline.
  • Tracker: The right panel shows milestones and discovered endings across runs.
Tips
  • Balance: Trust is critical—don’t rely solely on gifts. Mix Study and sincere Talk.
  • Focus: Switching focus between Saharsh and Anbu changes flavor and small outcomes; diversify to keep growth steady.
  • Difficulty: Hard raises gift prices and tightens pacing; Easy is more forgiving for experimenting.
Notes
  • Save/Load: Use the buttons in the footer for persistent runs.
  • Reset: Start a fresh run anytime.
  • Goal: Earn a mutual, respectful confession—no shortcuts. Your choices and timing make the moment.
