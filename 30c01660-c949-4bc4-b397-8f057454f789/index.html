A romantic visual novel game featuring Yussef and Saharsh, with a retro arcade mini-game and a shocking twist ending.

How to Play


  1. Dialogue: Click on the dialogue box (or press SPACE) to advance the story and conversations.

  2. Arcade Mini-Game:

    • When Yussef challenges Saharsh to the arcade game, the screen will switch to a Pac-Man style maze.

    • Controls: Use Arrow Keys to move Yussef (the yellow circle).

    • Goal: Collect all Pink Hearts to win Saharsh's love.

    • Avoid: The ghosts! If they touch you, the game ends (but the date continues with a different dialogue).



  3. The Ending: Experience the dramatic conclusion to the date.


Features
